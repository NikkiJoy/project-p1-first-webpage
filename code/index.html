Hi

I’m Nikki, an entrepreneur and artist dedicated to tech, culture and society. I am a co-founder of the responsible AI consultancy Intersections, the futuring advisory firm FutureSense and a member of the art collective t8y. Moreover, I’m a board member of HEK and CodeExcursion.

Ventures
